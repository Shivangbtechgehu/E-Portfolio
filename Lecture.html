Lecture 2 - 10/02/2025

Today's lecture was very interesting because sir played three mind games with us:

  • We had to do the opposite of the instructions given by sir.
  • We had to say a word similar to the last word spoken by someone else.
  • We had to describe the work of a pencil apart from writing.

Through these activities, I learned about the functioning of the left brain. The outcome of these games was that we improved our creativity, quick thinking, and ability to break habitual patterns. It helped us realize the importance of flexible thinking in problem-solving.
